About Me

I'm Lyle!

After working in advertising for years, I decided to make the jump to tech. I have been interested in computers ever since I can remember - which would be when my family purchased a Dell 1995 Windows PC (good times). As a millenial, I grew up with computers and was fascinated by them, and what they could do. Since making the jump to a new career, I have learned more than I could have imagined. I am a web developer adept in all stages of web development. Very knowledgeable in HTML, CSS, SASS, SQL, JAVA, Firebase, APIs, and React. Experience in UI, testing, creating apps, and debugging processes for clients. Bringing forth expertise in front end development, installation, testing and overall maintenance of web systems. I am equipped with a great diverse and promising skill-set. Able to self manage, but truly thrive in team environments with others and projects. LinkedIn
